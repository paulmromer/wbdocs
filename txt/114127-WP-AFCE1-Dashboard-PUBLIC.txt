TOP 5 ACTION/ATTENTION


  1. Uganda disbursement ratio is 16.3%. TTLs of Uganda projects requested to
     follow up on the slow disbursing projects and liaise with government

     for 12 months or more should be downgraded to MU.

     Meeting. Task Team Leaders are requested to take action to insert realistic
     timetable and schedule the PCN Meeting with respective CD/CM ASAP.

     delivered (see page 17-18). SL/TTLs are requested to review the list and
     ensure delivery.

     Country Office and Uganda Country Office exhausted their allocation.
